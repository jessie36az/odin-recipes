Churro Bombs

What is a Churro Bomb?

Churro Bombs are a delightful twist on traditional churros. They are small, round, and filled with all the deliciousness of churros but in a bite-sized form. These sweet treats are perfect for parties, gatherings, or as a special dessert.

These churro bombs are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, coated in a generous layer of cinnamon sugar. They are easy to make and even easier to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Oil for frying
  • Sugar and cinnamon for coating

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, salt, and butter. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add flour and stir until mixture forms a ball.
  3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Stir in vanilla extract.
  6. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Drop dough by tablespoonfuls into hot oil. Fry until golden brown.
  8. Drain on paper towels.
  9. Roll warm churro bombs in sugar and cinnamon mixture.
