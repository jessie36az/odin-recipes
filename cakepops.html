Cake Pops Recipe

What is a Cake Pop?

Cake Pops are a fun and creative way to enjoy cake. They are essentially small, bite-sized pieces of cake on a stick, coated in candy melts and decorated with sprinkles. Cake Pops are perfect for parties, celebrations, and as a delightful treat for any occasion.

These sweet treats are not only delicious but also customizable. You can use any flavor of cake and frosting, and decorate them to match any theme or color scheme. Cake Pops are sure to be a hit with both kids and adults!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of cake mix (any flavor)
  • 1 can of frosting (any flavor)
  • Candy melts
  • Cake pop sticks
  • Sprinkles for decoration

Instructions

  1. Bake the cake according to the package instructions. Let it cool completely.
  2. Crumble the cake into a large bowl.
  3. Add frosting and mix until well combined.
  4. Shape the mixture into balls and place on a baking sheet.
  5. Insert sticks into each ball and freeze for 30 minutes.
  6. Melt the candy melts according to package instructions.
  7. Dip each cake pop into the melted candy and decorate with sprinkles.
  8. Let them set and enjoy!
