What is a Filled Churro?

Filled churros are an exciting variation of the classic churro, offering a delicious surprise inside. These churros are filled with various types of fillings such as chocolate, caramel, or fruit jams. They are a popular treat that adds an extra layer of flavor and texture to the traditional churro experience.

The exterior remains crispy and coated with cinnamon sugar, while the inside bursts with your favorite filling. Perfect for dessert lovers who enjoy a little extra sweetness!
