    • h1>What is a Filled Churro?

    Filled churros are an exciting variation of the classic churro, offering a delicious surprise inside. These churros are filled with various types of fillings such as chocolate, caramel, or even fruit jams. They are a popular treat that adds an extra layer of flavor and texture to the traditional churro experience.

    The exterior of filled churros remains crispy and coated with cinnamon sugar, while the inside bursts with your favorite filling. They are perfect for dessert lovers who enjoy a little extra sweetness in every bite.

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup water
    • 2 tablespoons sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup butter
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 3 eggs
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Oil for frying
    • Sugar and cinnamon for coating
    • Filling of your choice (chocolate, caramel, etc.)

    Steps

    1. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, salt, and butter. Bring to a boil.
    2. Add flour and stir until mixture forms a ball.
    3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
    4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
    5. Stir in vanilla extract.
    6. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375°F (190°C).
    7. Pipe dough into hot oil using a pastry bag fitted with a star tip. Fry until golden brown.
    8. Drain on paper towels.
    9. Roll warm churros in sugar and cinnamon mixture.
    10. Fill each churro with your desired filling using a piping bag.
